How To Use

  1. Click "Select Images" and choose all your ID card photos.
  2. Click "Process" to automatically crop all images to the correct size.
  3. Review the images. Use the "Image Editing Tools" if any adjustments are needed.
  4. Use the "Enhance Options" buttons like "Auto Tone" to improve image quality.
  5. In the right panel, assign images as "Front" or "Back". Use "Auto-Assign" for large batches.
  6. Click "Generate PDF" to save or "Print Directly" to finish!
